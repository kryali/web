--- layout: post title: Chrome OS Tablet UI Concept tags: - Technology status: pending type: post published: false meta: {} --- After the dust of the iPad settled, chromium devs released a concept of a chrome OS tablet. The video shows a pretty simple and minimalistic UI.  All the apps pop up in a launcher, triggered by a long touch, eliminating reducing the clutter on the desktop. The design looks fantastic. Unlike the iPad, the concept shows off the ability to multitask. Multitasking is a feature that really shines with multitouch. The fact that the iPad shows no plans for such design is a great dissapointment. Chrome OS, has a great potential to succeed in the market because of the growing availability of mobile internet. Let's just hope that google holds to its unofficial  motto: "Don't Be Evil"
