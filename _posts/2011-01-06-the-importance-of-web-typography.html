--- layout: post title: The Importance of Web Typography tags: - design - typography status: pending type: post published: false meta: {} --- As the web is a medium based highly on reading, not enough web designers take the initiative to study the way in which people read content on their websites. Typography is a largely overlooked aspect of web design, and a very important one at that. Allowing easy readability is essential to creating a good user interface. Readers should enjoy reading your content, not struggle to do so. Web typography is a great resource for explaining the theory behind styling text. The creator, Richard Rutter, outlines many of the common errors designers make when styling their content. Organizing web content in a proper manner positively influences the user's experience. Read Rutter's tips as you are designing your new site and you will be astounded by the impact his tips has on it. Look at this blog, I follow a lot of useful typographical conventions.
